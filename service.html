Mobile Grooming

For convenience, Furry Fresh Grooming also offers mobile services that come to your doorstep! The mobile van is fully equipped with everything needed to give your dog a full spa treatment right outside your home.

Hours:

Monday-Saturday: 8:00am - 5:00pm

Our mobile grooming services bring the salon experience to your doorstep! For each of our standard services, we charge an additional fee for mobile grooming:

  • Within 5 miles of our salon: $25
  • 5-10 miles from our salon: +$35
  • 10-15 miles from our salon: +$45
  • 15-20 miles from our salon: +$55

Additional Mobile Grooming Notes:

  • A minimum charge of $75 applies to all mobile grooming appointments.
  • Appointments may be subject to a fuel surcharge for distances greater than 20 miles from our salon.
  • Mobile services are subject to availability and may require booking further in advance than in-salon appointments.
  • Cancellation policy: 24-hour notice required to avoid a $50 cancellation fee.
